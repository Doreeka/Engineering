Chemical engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with chemical production and the manufacture of products through chemical processes. A day or two after learning about chemical engineering, we made oobleck. Oobleck is a non-utonium liquid, meaning that it is solid when force is applied, and liquid when it is still. We made it by putting corn starch in a bowl then slowly adding water and mixing until it became the consistency we desired. I had lots of fun making and playing with oobleck, and I would recomend it to anyone.

    7. 3/4/2024 - This week we started a new project. The project is an egg drop. The goal is to drop an egg without breaking it. Specifically, we are going to drop it from two and a half stories. This will be done in teams of three. My team and I have thought of a design that we think would keep the egg un-shattered. We will make a rectagular cardboard box and completely surround it with straws. We will also attach a trash bag or two to the top of it to slow it down when falling. Inside the box, we will put baloons at the top and bottom to cushion the egg which is in the middle. Next to the egg, there will also be cotton balls. I am scared of the trash bag ripping and the straws breaking. If the trash bag rips, then the box will fall extremely fast, and if the straws break, then the box will directly hit the ground. The point of the straws are to cushion the fall when it hits the ground. I think the weakest part of our design is the straws.
